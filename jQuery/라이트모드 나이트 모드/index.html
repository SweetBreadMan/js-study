Light Mode & Dark Mode

Light mode is characterized by a bright and refreshing design, utilizing white or light-colored backgrounds to provide a visually vibrant experience. This mode is commonly used during the day and features crisp typography and excellent readability, ensuring clear presentation of content.

Dark mode, on the other hand, consists of a dark background with bright text, offering a modern and sophisticated feel while avoiding strain on the eyes. It is primarily used during nighttime or in dimly lit environments, providing a visually comfortable environment even with low light or weak lighting conditions. Additionally, dark mode reduces energy consumption, enhances screen uniformity, and helps reduce eye fatigue.
